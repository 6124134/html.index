Tahu bodo

Ingredients

  • 5 yellow tofu
  • 7 red chillies
  • 2 garlics
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 flavoring
  • 1 tbsp sugar

Cook

  1. Fried tofu and garlic (half cooked)
  2. Grind red chillies, garlics, salt, sugar and seasoning
  3. Add 1 tbsp of hot oil used for frying the tofu, then coarsely grind the tofu with the chili sauce
  4. The tahu bodo is ready to be served
